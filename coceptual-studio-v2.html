Common Questions

Interior Design FAQs for Ranchi

Everything you need to know before starting your interior project with Coceptual Studio.

A complete 2BHK interior in Ranchi typically costs between ₹5 lakh and ₹14 lakh depending on materials and scope. Modular kitchen + 2 bedrooms + living room with false ceiling can be done in the ₹7–10 lakh range with mid-range materials. We provide free, itemised quotes before any work begins.

A complete 2BHK interior project typically takes 45–60 days from design approval to handover. A single modular kitchen takes 15–21 days. Turnkey projects for 3BHK or larger may take 60–90 days. We sign a project timeline with you and stick to it.

Modular kitchen pricing in Ranchi starts from ₹1.2 lakh for a basic laminate finish and goes up to ₹4–6 lakh for premium acrylic or membrane finishes. Cost depends on kitchen size, layout, shutters, countertop material and hardware quality. We give a per-sq-ft quote after a site visit.

Yes. Coceptual Studio has active project teams in Jamshedpur and Bokaro. We handle full-home interiors, modular kitchens, wardrobes and office commercial interiors in both cities. For Gumla, we take selected large-scale projects.

Turnkey means we handle everything — design, civil work, electrical, false ceiling, flooring, carpentry, painting and furnishing. You hand us the bare space and we return a complete, ready-to-live-in home. One team, one point of contact, one contract.

Yes. All our modular furniture, wardrobes and carpentry work comes with a 5-year warranty. Hardware is covered under manufacturer warranty. We also offer an annual maintenance service for post-warranty upkeep.
